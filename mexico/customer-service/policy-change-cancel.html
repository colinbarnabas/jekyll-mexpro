Mexican Auto Insurance Policy Changes

Changes/Endorsements/Cancellations at a Glance

In order to change your policy We MUST receive the following in writing:

  1. Cancellation Requests

    1. Must include policy number, name of insured, and reason for cancellation

  2. Transfer of coverage to second vehicle (one-time vehicle change)

    1. Only available for six and 12 month policies.

    2. Must include year, make, model, VIN, license plate, and value.

  3. Adding a towed unit or trailer

    1. Only available for six and 12 month policies.

    2. Must include year, make, model, VIN, license plate, and value.

Please email these requests to Mexico@Mexpro.com.


Endorsement Requests/Service that can be done over the phone:

  1. Name corrections

  2. Vehicle corrections

    1. Excludes the value or any change that would change the premium of the policy

  3. Vehicle corrections

    1. Excludes the value or any change that would change the premium of the policy

  4. Effective date changes

    1. Policy cannot yet be in effect

  5. Add Drivers

    1. Must have a valid US or Canadian license and be over 21 (unless this surcharge has been added to the policy prior to issuing)

  6. Edit Lien holder information

  7. Providing copy of policy


Hours of Operation:

Monday-Friday 7:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m., Saturday 7:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m. Arizona Mountain Standard Time (MST) (year round). Voicemail and email available after-hours.
