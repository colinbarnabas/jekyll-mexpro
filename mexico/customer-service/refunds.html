Mexico Car Insurance Policy Refunds

Refunds for cancelled Mexico insurance policies will be credited back to credit card with which the original policy was purchased. This may take a day or two depending on your credit card company/bank, and on their processing times.

For all Mexico car insurance policies, the refund will be credited to the credit card charged. Depending on your credit card company, you should see the refund within 72 hours. All refunds will be your return premium less a cancellations fee. This fee will apply to all cancellations unless an exception has been made. The cancellation fee is always half of the agency fee.
