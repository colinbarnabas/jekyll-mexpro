Why you need special Mexico Insurance

In Mexico it is the law to have Mexico Insurance from a Mexico company that will cover you for any potential liability claims, and your current policy stops at the border and will not cover you or your vehicle for any potential claims or damages to your vehicle.

Liability

Liability car insurance covers damages to another person or other property resulting from an accident you cause. Our Liability only policies and Driver License policies are one of the most basic types of auto insurance coverage, and are the minimum required to have when driving in Mexico.

Full coverage

Full coverage refers to Liability coverages, plus coverage to you and your vehicle. Our Standard and Premium policies will not only protect your vehicle as well as damages to others, our Premium policies have the most comprehensive Mexico insurance coverages available.

Claims

In Mexico an adjuster is usually your first point of contact. If you are in an accident, or need to file a claim, immediately call the 01 800 number listed on your policy. An adjuster will come to you (he will need your policy). He will write a claim report (and provide you with a copy), and depending on the situation, will provide you with any further instructions. For more information about the claim process.

Temporary vehicle permits

If you plan to drive a vehicle beyond the Mexico "Free Zone" you will need to purchase a Mexico Vehicle Import Permit. The temporary vehicle import permit can be obtained from Banjercito, the official Mexican issuing agency, and may be purchased online at the Banjercito website or at the Mexican border.
