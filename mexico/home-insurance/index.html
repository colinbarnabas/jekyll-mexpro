Underwritten By

Mexpro has earned a great reputation for providing outstanding coverage for homes and condos in Mexico. Our homeowners insurance is underwritten by one of the best known and most trusted insurance companies in Mexico, Grupo Nacional Provincial. For over 100 years, GNP has been the prremier provider of insurance in Mexio and holds and A- rating from Global Rating Service A.M. Best.

Quote and Purchase Online

You can use our easy online application, in English or Spanish, to quote and purchase your policy.

Eligibility

We can bind homes and condos online up to $ 1 million in dwelling replacement value. For homes over $ 1 million in value, please call our office (855-Mexpro1).

NOTE: We are currently unable to insure homes in the Yucatan Peninsula.

Choices at competitive prices

We have choices for you. You can purchase a policy simply to satisfy Mexico Legal Requirements, such as our Liability Only or Driver License policies or you can purchase the broadest possible coverage such as our Full Coverage Standard or Premium Policies.

On our quick and easy quoting application, you can get up to 12 quotes to compare from. Just pick the policy which is best for you and print your policy in less than 10 minutes!

Liability

Liability car insurance covers damages to another person or other property resulting from an accident you cause. Liability only policies are the most basic auto insurance coverage, and the minimum required to have when driving in Mexico. There are no coverages to your vehicle with this policy.

Full coverage

Full coverage refers to liability coverages plus coverage for you and your vehicle. We offer a bare bone 'Basic' policy that is competitively priced and we offer, and recommend, our comprehensive 'Premium' policy. Our 'Premium' policy is a rare find with high limits, low deductibles and multiple extra coverages that are not included in most traditional Mexico insurance policies.
