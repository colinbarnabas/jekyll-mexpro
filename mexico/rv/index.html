Mexican RV Insurance

Easy, Affordable, Quality RV Coverage in Mexico

Whether you are driving an RV to Mexico for the weekend or the winter, you need insurance.

With Mexpro you have the ability to purchase the Mexican RV Insurance online that best suits your needs. We offer policies on a daily basis or in six month or annual terms. Price is primarily based on these factors:

Also,

  • All Repairs and Claims can be settled in US or Canada.
  • Caravan Discounts are available, call us to inquire.

A recreational vehicle with a high value can be expensive to insure in Mexico. To reduce this expense, it may be best to add a "Mexico Physical Damage" endorsement to your U.S. insurance policy. This endorsement covers physical damage in Mexico. It is the best and least expensive way to insure your RV in Mexico.

If your insurer doesn't offer a Mexico endorsement, you may want to contact Mexpro. We can provide a list of U.S. insurers who extend physical damage insurance coverage for Mexico for a very low premium. This option can create big savings on your RV insurance policy in Mexico!

If you are Canadian and cannot take advantage of these savings, it's still advantageous to check our rates, as we continually work with our carriers to lower them.
