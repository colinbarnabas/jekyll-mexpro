Short Term US Tourist Auto Insurance Coverage

The United States and Mexico's legal systems are VERY different. The filing of a lawsuit will be the first thing to occur after an accident in the US. Therefore it is important that you have an insurance policy that will cover third party liability, and that will cover the costs incurred, in your defense, in the event a claim is filed. It is of utmost importance to have a US insurance policy that will cover you while you drive a Mexico vehicle in the US or Canada.

The National Unity insurance policy from Mexpro provides coverage for damages to third parties for which you may be liable. This includes personal damages, as well as damages to the property of others. The policy also covers defense costs that may arise in the event of a lawsuit caused by a covered loss.

There are also two more coverage options included: The first is coverage for medical expenses incurred by the occupants of your vehicle, due to a covered accident. The second is roadside assistance. Imagine if your vehicle broke down in a foreign country. With roadside assistance you are provided towing, the jump-starting of your vehicle, flat tire service, and locksmith services.

It is important to note that this policy does NOT cover damages to your vehicle, but only damages to third parties.
