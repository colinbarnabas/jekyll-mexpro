Mexican Auto Insurance Options & Comparisons

When it comes to insurance there two important factors: coverage and cost. Mexican Auto Insurance is no different. Mexpro offers a number of options to ensure you can find a policy that fits your needs. Of the four companies Mexpro represents, each offers two programs: a standard policy—what you might find at the border, and an extended policy. The extended policy offers a better level of coverage, while the standard policy provides an economical way to insure your vehicle in Mexico. Below find the breakdown of each of the coverage options and limits each companies offers. While coverage is relatively similar, be sure to focus on the limits, as there are subtle differences.
