Extended Car Insurance Coverage Comparisons

Purchasing the extended Mexico Insurance policy package is a great idea, as it provides significant benefits over the standard policy. And it provides the peace of mind, knowing you purchased the best Mexican auto insurance available in the market. However, each extended package is a little different in the coverage it provides. To ensure you purchase the coverage to meet your needs, please look at the information below where we have broken down coverage side-by-side to provide a comparison of each program.
