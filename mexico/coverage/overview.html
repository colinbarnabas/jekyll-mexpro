Auto Insurance Coverage Options

Mexpro's Auto Insurance policies are offered with several different levels of coverage. Our customers can purchase basic liability, standard coverage, our best extended or upgraded coverage and travel assistance.

  • Basic Liability Package

    A basic liability package offers minimal coverage and is the least expensive option available. Commonly purchased for older vehicles (1978 or older), or those that have values that are less than $3,000.00. Basic liability coverage includes the following:

    • Third Party Liability
      • Bodily Injury Liability
      • Property Damage Liability

    • Legal Assistance
      • Bail Bonds
      • Legal Aid
      • Impound Expenses

    • Medical Payments
      • For the driver and occupants of the vehicle

    • Limited Towing / Roadside Assistance
      • Towing in the event of a covered loss
      • Roadside labor

  • Standard Coverage

    • Collisions
    • Overturning
    • Glass Breakage
    • Fire
    • Lightning / Electrical Storms
    • Explosion
    • Forces of Nature
    • Strikes and Riots
    • Transportation of the Insured Vehicle
    • Towing Expenses
    • Theft of the Insured Vehicle

  • Extended Coverage

    Our Extended Policy is the most complete coverage package we offer and it is also our most popular seller! Purchasing an extended policy attaches an endorsement to the standard package, which adds the following coverage and benefits:

    • Partial Theft
    • Vandalism
    • Fixed Deductibles (this is also an available optional with the Standard Coverage package)
    • Minimum limit of liability coverage is automatically increased
    • US Labor Rates / Increased Cost of Repair
    • Uninsured Motorist Deductible Waiver or Increased Medical Payments
    • GAP Coverage - ABA Seguros & El Aguila have added GAP coverage up to 15% over the Actual Cash Value of the vehicle

  • MexVisit Travel Assistance

    While each of our insurers offer a basic towing package, our MexVisit travel assistance package offers an inexpensive optional policy upgrade with most of our insurers. ABA automatically includes MexVisit as part of their most basic policy option. MexVisit Travel Assistance coverage includes the following and much more:

    • Medical Transfers / Evacuation
      • Emergency Land or Air Evacuation to closest qualified Medical Facility

    • Return Tickets Home
      • In the event of an accident or mechanical breakdown, arranges and pays for the transportation of the named insured and up to four travel companions, in coach class (air or land) to their place of permanent residence.

    • Roadside Assistance
      • Emergency Fuel Supply
      • Flat Tire Service
      • Jumpstart Service
      • Locksmith Service
      • Towing in event of Mechanical Breakdown (up to $300.00 and/or two events)
