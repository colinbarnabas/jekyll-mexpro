Travel Medical Insurance

Protects you in the event of an illness or injury when traveling outside of your country of residence. It provides key medical benefits in case of an emergency.

Travel Medical coverage is ideal for:

  • International vacationers
  • Relatives visiting from overseas
  • People going on cruises, safaris or guided tours
  • International business travelers
  • Students studying abroad

Vehicle Temporary Import Permit (TIP)

If you plan to drive a vehicle beyond the Mexico "Free Zone" you will need to purchase a Vehicle Temporary Import Permit (TIP). The TIP can be obtained from Banjercito, the official Mexican issuing agency, and may be purchased online at the Banjercito website or at a check point in Mexico.
