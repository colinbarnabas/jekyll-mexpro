Why you need special Mexico Insurance

Responsabilidad Civil

El seguro de automóvil de responsabilidad civil cubre daños a otras personas u otra propiedad como resultado de un accidente debido a su culpa. Pólizas de solo Responsabilidad Civil lleva la cobertura de seguro de auto más básica y la mínima requerida mientras el conduce en Mexico. No hay cobertura para su vehículo con esta póliza.

Cobertura Completa

Cobertura Completa se refiere a las coberturas de responsabilidad civil, más cobertura para usted y su vehículo. Ofrecemos una póliza de huesos desnudos 'Básica' que tiene un precio competitivo y ofrecemos, y recomendamos, nuestra póliza integral 'Premium'. Nuestra póliza 'Premium' es un hallazgo raro con altos límites, deducibles bajos y múltiples coberturas adicionales que no están incluidas en las pólizas de seguros más tradicionales de Mexico.

Liability

Liability car insurance covers damages to another person or other property resulting from an accident you cause. Liability only policies are the most basic auto insurance coverage, and the minimum required to have when driving in Mexico. There are no coverages to your vehicle with this policy.

Full coverage

Full coverage refers to liability coverages plus coverage for you and your vehicle. We offer a bare bone 'Basic' policy that is competitively priced and we offer, and recommend, our comprehensive 'Premium' policy. Our 'Premium' policy is a rare find with high limits, low deductibles and multiple extra coverages that are not included in most traditional Mexico insurance policies.

Claims

In Mexico an adjuster is usually your first point of contact. If you are in an accident, or need to file a claim, immediately call the 01 800 number listed on your policy. An adjuster will come to you (he will need your policy). He will write a claim report (and provide you with a copy), and depending on the situation, will provide you with any further instructions. Temporary vehicle permits

If you plan to drive a vehicle beyond the Mexico "Free Zone" you will need to purchase a Mexico Vehicle Import Permit. The temporary vehicle import permit can be obtained from Banjercito, the official Mexican issuing agency, and may be purchased online at the Banjercito website or at the Mexican border.
